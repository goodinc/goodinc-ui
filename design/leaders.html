Society

In 2014, GOOD launched a new support community for leaders with a goal of empowering a community of impact professionals through real talk. We believe we’re all in this pursuit together and by sharing our struggles, our wins, and our stories—we will be more impactful and inspired to push forward.

The GOOD Society: Leaders is an invite-only community of over 150 leaders from the business world, foundations, nonprofits and creative fields. We come together as local chapters in four major U.S. cities (NYC, LA, SF & DC) as well as for select national gatherings to connect, meditate and ideate about doing impactful work. Our format is flexible, shining a light on the hard work of nonprofits, corporations, creative individuals, and foundations alike.

Want to join The GOOD Society?

With a membership to The GOOD Society, you’ll get our quarterly journal, invites to do-good events around the U.S., and discounts on sustainable products.
